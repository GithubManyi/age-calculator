Is my birth date stored anywhere?

No. All calculations happen locally in your browser. We don't store your birth date on any servers.

How accurate are the planetary ages?

Planetary ages are calculated using average orbital periods. They're accurate for entertainment and educational purposes.

Can I calculate age for past or future dates?

Yes! Use the "Calculate Age As Of" field to select any date in the past or future.

What if I was born on February 29th?

Leap year birthdays are handled correctly. Your age is calculated based on the actual calendar days.

Can I use this for legal documents?

No. AgeMaster is for entertainment and informational purposes only. Use official documents for legal age verification.

Is this free to use?

Yes! AgeMaster is completely free with no hidden fees or subscriptions.
